LEARN SOME FACTS ABOUT UFA

There are 85 regions in Russia, 21 of which are Republics. Bashkortostan is one of them.
A republic has its own government, president, constitution as well as the capital.
Republics are more independent unlike other regions.
Ufa is the capital of Bashkortostan. Its population is a bit more than 1 million people.
It's pretty big and developped. There's no metro. The main means of transport is a bus, public or private, and taxi.
The city was situated in 1574. You can learn about its history in the National Museum.
There are many green parks in the city. It's quite convenient to go sightseeing by bicycle in summer time.
